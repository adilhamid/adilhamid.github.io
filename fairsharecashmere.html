Fairshare Cashmere

A Heaven for all the stakeholders of Cashmere and Cashmere products.

Mission

Fairshare’s mission is to support artisans and enhance their financial wellness in a socially responsible manner. We’re committed to providing the finest quality Cashmere to our customers. Our goal is to inspire and empower people to lead a simple act of buying Cashmere sourced from the place of its origin.

Vision

We envision to bring back the fair share of Cashmere industry to Kashmir, the birthplace of brand Cashmere. Our vision is to increase the global market share of Kashmiri Cashmere and create sustainable job opportunities for local, otherwise unemployed, youth.
